Places to visit in Phuket

Phuket Big Buddha, or The Great Buddha of Phuket, is a seated Maravija Buddha statue in Phuket, Thailand. The official name is Phra Phutta Ming Mongkol Eknakiri, shortened to Ming Mongkol Buddha. Sitting atop Nakkerd Hill near Chalong, construction began in 2004. Expansion of the base was ongoing as of 2015.

 

Rumored to be the most beautiful beach in all of Thailand, Freedom Beach is a scenic oasis. A 300-meter expanse of white sand beach sits between the verdant, jungle-covered hills and the bright blue, clear-as-can-be water of the Andaman Sea.

The most important of the 29 buddhist temples of Phuket is Wat Chalong or formally Wat Chaiyathararam, located in the Chalong Subdistrict, Mueang Phuket District. 

Verdant park for rescued elephants where visitors can feed & bathe the animals on guided tours.
